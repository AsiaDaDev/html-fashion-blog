Lena's 2021 Fashion Insights

Being fashionable doesn't take much. It's all about keeping things balanced and showcasing you're individuality.
However, if you still feel a bit lost check out some quick tricks to looking good no matter what.

Black Face Masks a Must

Face masks have become a necessary accessory within the last year. Due to the fact that a face mask won't harm you, and can even be a helpful addition if you're suffering from a little confidence issues, there's no reason not to stock up.
Unless you're going for a coordinated color scheme then consider choosing a black face mask on your next outing. Black masks not only go with anything you may be wearing but they can even help your face look a little slimmer.

Belt Trumps Baggy

Considering sagging your pants around town? Well, you may want to reconsider.
Sagging may have been a common fashion trend in the early 2000s, but its fallen out of favor with most lately. Instead of risking you're pants falling to your ankles grab a properly sized belt with a plain or stylized buckle, depending on your preference.
